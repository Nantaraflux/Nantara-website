Our Story

Born from watching orders fall through the cracks.

Nantara AI started with a simple observation: some of the hardest-working suppliers in Southeast Asia were losing revenue not because their products weren't good enough, but because their order systems couldn't keep up.

WhatsApp messages piling up. Excel sheets with conflicting data. Buyers waiting hours — sometimes days — for a simple price confirmation. Repeat customers treated like strangers because there was no buyer history to reference.

"We didn't want to build another tool. We wanted to build a system that makes every other tool work together."

So we built Nantara: an order intelligence layer that sits on top of the tools suppliers already use — WhatsApp, spreadsheets, CRMs — and connects them into one automated pipeline powered by AI.

Today, we serve B2B suppliers across Indonesia and beyond, helping them reclaim 40+ hours per week and never lose another order.
