The Problem

Why B2B Suppliers Are Drowning in Order Chaos

Orders scattered everywhere

WhatsApp, email, Tokopedia, Shopee. No single source of truth. Orders get missed, duplicated, or delayed.

Manual data entry kills your team

Buyer message WA → write to Excel → copy to Airtable → input to fulfillment → notify buyer. 4 systems, 40+ hours/week wasted.

Repeat orders = missed upsell

Buyer ordered before? No context. You re-quote at old price. Lose upsell. Buyer thinks you overcharge. They leave.
